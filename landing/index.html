Our Mission

FortressBackup was built on a simple premise: Your data belongs to you. In an era of increasing cloud reliance and data fragmentation, we provide the tools to take back control.

We combine the robustness of enterprise-grade backup tools (like Borg and Restic) with a beautiful, modern interface. No complex command-line scripts, no vendor lock-in, just pure, unbreakable data protection that you control entirely.
